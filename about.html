About DriveMo

DriveMo serves as a platform pairing drivers with clients who do not wish or are unable to drive their own vehicles. To book a driver for daily office commutes, mid-day shopping, night-outs, airport transfers, or weekend getaways.

With our app, users can hire drivers either on an hourly basis or for specific drop location trips. First, they choose whether the trip will be one-way or round-trip. Then, the rider selects the type of car they need driven, which helps match them with suitable drivers nearby.

Users can also schedule drivers in advance to ensure reliable transportation for important destinations, such as office meetings or visa appointments, where timely arrival is crucial. The app ensures quick and convenient pairing with vetted drivers based on the user's specific needs, providing a smooth and personalized experience.
